## **Step 1**

The first step is downloading [arduino IDE](https://www.arduino.cc/en/software/)

## **Step 2**

The Second step is choosing the right board and port. For this you will look in device manager for the port info that your esp32-cam has, after that you will go in arduino IDE Tool->Boards->Board-manager in the board manager look for esp 32  and choose the one that says"esp32 by Esspresif systems".
